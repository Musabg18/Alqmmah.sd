About Alqmmah Technology

34+ years of excellence in telecom and infrastructure solutions

Company Overview

Delivering premium contracting services since 1989

Alqmmah Technology LTD was established to provide high-quality services in telecommunications, energy, construction, and commercial solutions. Our company consists of two entities:

  • Alqmmah Technology LTD. Company - Focused on technology solutions
  • Alqmmah Contracting Business Name - Specializing in construction and infrastructure

With over three decades of experience, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and innovation in all our service areas.

Our Vision

Building an outstanding reputation through high-quality services that add real value to the service market, establishing ourselves as the preferred partner for telecom and infrastructure solutions in the region.

Our Mission

To provide reliable services across all sectors with high responsiveness, particularly in telecommunications. We commit to delivering solutions that exceed client expectations through innovation, expertise, and dedication.
